About Shilpa

Founder & Artist

Founder of International Art Studio is an Architect - Urban Planner by professional qualification and now working full time Artist and Art Teacher. She is also a mother of two children. In her art studio you will find her working on illustrations, water colours, oil pastels, pencil shading and acrylic paintings. She has been drawing as long as she can remember. Her teaching experience started in architecture college where she was a teacher in Architectural graphics studio. She also has experience in teaching younger children, Mommy and Me, as well as Middle School and High School kids. When she is not inventing new and exciting classes for her Art Studio, you can find her playing with her children and cooking food for her family.
